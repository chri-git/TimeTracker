# TimeTracker
A simple Android app for tracking working hours. Enter the time of your arrival at work and the time you finish, and the app will compute your time balance for you.

## Cheat Sheet
| Question                        | Answer                                                        |
|:--------------------------------|:--------------------------------------------------------------|
